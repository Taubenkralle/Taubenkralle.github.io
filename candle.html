Privacy Policy

The app "Candle" does not collect, store, or process any personal data.

No user accounts are created, no tracking tools are used
and no data is shared with third parties.

The app works completely offline.
If an internet connection is used (e.g., for system functions),
this happens exclusively via the interfaces provided by the operating system.
